## Score Interpretation

| Score | Meaning                                                                        |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 0     | Not demonstrated / unsafe / incomplete                                         |
| 1     | Partial competence, requires support                                           |
| 2     | Fully competent, leads independently                                           |
| 3     | Exceptional, drives architecture, mentors others, anticipates risks, innovates |

---

## Notes for Interviewer

* Senior candidates must demonstrate **architectural thinking**, not just coding.
* Focus on AI governance, security, and system-level trade-offs.
* Include scenario-based exercises: scaling, failure recovery, and ethical AI decisions.
* Leadership and mentoring examples are essential.

---

> This rubric is suitable for hiring senior engineers or assessing candidates for team lead and principal engineer roles in an AI-era software organization.
