# Weekly Self-Audit — Bridge Engineer

> **Purpose**: Maintain engineering sharpness in an AI-accelerated environment.
>
> This is not a productivity tracker. It is a **judgment and ownership audit**.

Complete this **once per week**. Be brutally honest.

---

## Week Metadata

* **Week:** ______________________
* **Primary Project / Focus:** ______________________
* **Role This Week:** Builder / Reviewer / Firefighter / Architect

---

## 1. System Awareness (The Bridge)

Answer in full sentences.

* What *system* did I work on this week (not files, not tickets)?
* What upstream/downstream dependencies did I touch?
* What assumptions did I rely on that I did not personally verify?

**Score (0–3):** ☐0 ☐1 ☐2 ☐3

---

## 2. AI Usage Audit

Be precise.

* Where did I use AI this week?
* Did I treat AI output as **drafts or decisions**?
* Did I catch at least one AI mistake or hallucination?

> If you cannot name a mistake AI made, you likely trusted it too much.

**Score (0–3):** ☐0 ☐1 ☐2 ☐3

---

## 3. Code Quality & Ownership

* What code do I fully understand end-to-end?
* What code could I *not* explain to another engineer?
* Where did I trade correctness for speed?

**Score (0–3):** ☐0 ☐1 ☐2 ☐3

---

## 4. Failure & Risk Thinking

* What could have failed in production this week?
* What guardrails prevented it?
* What guardrails were missing?

**Score (0–3):** ☐0 ☐1 ☐2 ☐3

---

## 5. Communication & Decision-Making

* What decision did I clearly explain *why*, not just *what*?
* Where did ambiguity remain unresolved?
* Did I document at least one meaningful decision?

**Score (0–3):** ☐0 ☐1 ☐2 ☐3

---

## Weekly Summary

* **Strongest Area This Week:** ______________________
* **Weakest Area This Week:** ______________________
* **One Concrete Improvement Next Week:** ______________________

---

## Red Flags (Circle Any)

* I merged code I didn’t fully understand
* I deferred thinking to AI
* I optimized locally without system awareness
* I avoided hard conversations

> Two consecutive weeks with the same red flag = **mandatory deep review**.

---

> *“Weekly excellence is not intensity. It is consistency under scrutiny.”*
